1- VEG TAWA SANDWICH RECIPE :  

Delicious Kids friendly sandwich recipe – without cheese

Prep Time Cook Time Total Time
15 mins 25 mins 40 mins

Ingredients

  • 10 bread slices
  • Butter as needed to toast
  • ¼ cup Green chutney

For the vegetable masala

  • 3 tsp oil
  • 2 tbsp chopped garlic
  • 2 finely chopped onions
  • 1 green chili
  • ¼ cup finely chopped carrot
  • ¼ cup finely chopped cabbage
  • ¼ cup finely chopped capsicum
  • ¼ cup finely chopped cauliflower
  • 1 cup boiled and mashed potato
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • ½ tsp garam masala
  • Salt as needed
  • Few slices cucumber sliced into roundels
  • 1 tsp chaat masala
  • You can add peas sweet corn and finely chopped broccoli and baby corn too.

Instructions

  1. In a pan add oil and add the chopped garlic and chili.
  2. Saute this for a minute and add the onions.
  3. Add a little salt and saute this till translucent.
  4. Now add in the carrot, cabbage, cauliflower and capsicum.
  5. Saute this for a minute.
  6. Add salt, red chili powder, and garam masala.
  7. Now add the mashed potato.
  8. Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  9. Switch off the flame.
  10. If needed you can add lemon juice just after switching off the flame.
  11. The sandwich filling is ready. The same filling can be used for making mixed veg paratha too.
  12. Take a slice of bread and apply green chutney on one side.
  13. You can use ketchup or chili sauce too if you want.
  14. Spread the filling on it.
  15. Place 4-5 sliced cucumbers on it. This is optional but it gives a nice taste.
  16. Sprinkle 2 pinches of chaat masala on the top.
  17. Take another slice of bread and apply green chutney. You can apply tomato ketchup or sweet chutney too.
  18. Place it on the top of the filling spread bread.
  19. Add little butter to a pan.
  20. Place the sandwich on it.
  21. Toast on 2 sides until golden brown.
  22. Add a little more butter while toasting.
  23. Alternatively, you can toast this on a sandwich maker too.
  24. Repeat the same for the rest of the bread and filling.
  25. Vegetable Tawa sandwich is ready.
  26. You can cut them into 2 and serve this.

Notes

  1. You can use sweet corn, peas, baby corn, and broccoli also.
  2. Potato can be replaced with sweet potato.
  3. Cheese slice or grated cheese can be added to the top of the cucumber.
  4. You can use white bread or wheat bread for making this Veg Tawa sandwich.




2 - MASALA OATS RECIPE :  

Prep Time Cook Time Total Time
5 mins 15 mins 20 mins

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup oats quick cooking oats
  • 1/4 cup vegetables I used finely chopped carrot, frozen peas thawed
  • 1/4 cup onion finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp tomato finely chopped
  • 1/2 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala powder
  • 1/4 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tbsp coriander leaves
  • 2 and 1/2 cups water
  • salt as required

To temper

  • 2 tsp oil
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan – when it is heated add cumin seeds let it splutter.
  2. Add onion, tomato and ginger garlic paste.
  3. Saute until it shrinks and tomatoes turn mushy.
  4. Now add vegetables I added carrot and frozen peas.
  5. Add turmeric powder, red chilli powder, coriander powder and garam masala powder along with required salt.
  6. Give a quick saute. Saute for 2 mins.
  7. Add water, give a quick mix .
  8. Let it come to a boil.
  9. Cook covered until the veggies are cooked soft.
  10. Now add oats after checking the vegetables(if they are cooked soft).
  11. Give a quick mix.
  12. Let it cook for a minute.
  13. Starts to boil,keep cooking.
  14. Turns thick more like porridge consistency.
  15. It should be flowing and sticky. Switch off at this stage as it may turn thick after cooling down.
  16. Finally garnish with coriander leaves.
  17. Give a quick mix and enjoy it hot! Serve masala oats hot!

3- RAVA UPMA RECIPE :  

Prep Time Cook Time Total Time
10 mins 20 mins 30 mins

Ingredients

  • Rava | sooji 1 cup
  • Water 2 – 2 and 1/2 cup
  • Oil 1 tblsp
  • Mustard seeds 1/4 tsp
  • Urad dal 1/4 tsp
  • Channa dal 1/4 tsp
  • Onion 1
  • Green chili 1
  • Cashew nuts few (optional)
  • Curry leaves few
  • Salt as needed
  • Ghee few drops (optional)

Instructions

  1. Dry roast the rava in a pan keeping it in a low flame. Be careful not to change the colour and at the same time ensure it is roasted properly. If not roasted well upma will not turn out fluffy.
  2. Keep the roasted rava aside.
  3. Chop the onions finely and slit the green chili.
  4. In the same pan add oil and add in the mustard seeds, urad dal, channa dal, green chili,curry leaves and cashew nuts(if adding)
  5. When the dals turn golden brown add the finely chopped onions.
  6. When the onions turn pink add 2 cups of water to this. Add salt. Allow this to come to a rolling boil.
  7. Meanwhile keep 1/2 cup of hot water ready by the side. Generally for rava upma it takes 2 cups of water for 1 cup of rava but it differs from brand to brand. Some rava will absorb more water.
  8. So in the last if needed we can add this hot water
  9. Lower the flame now and add the roasted rava to this and mix well without any lumps. If you keep the flame high and add rava it will form lumps.
  10. Once this is mixed well, you could make out whether it needs more water ot not. If you see sooji still not cooked add 1/4 cup of water, mix well and cover with a lid and keep it in low flame for 3 minutes.
  11. Take out the lid and mix well and fluff it up with a fork.
  12. Rava upma is ready to serve now.
  13. I love to eat with pickle with a hot cup of tea or coffee aside. Even with sugar also it will be very tasty.



4- CHOCOLATE MILKSHAKE RECIPE:  

Prep Time Cook Time Total Time
10 mins 5 mins 15 mins

Ingredients

  • 6 cups vanilla ice cream or chocolate ice cream
  • 1/3 cup milk , or more as needed, or use chocolate milk
  • 1/3 cup chocolate sauce
  • whipped cream , optional, for topping

Equipment

  • Blender

Instructions

  1. Combine ice cream, milk and chocolate sauce in a blender and blend until combined. I like my shakes really thick, but if you need to thin it, add a little more milk.
  2. Top with whipped cream.

5- MIXED VEG PARATHA RECIPE :  

Prep Time Cook Time Total Time
30 mins 30 mins 60 mins

Ingredients

  • 1&1/2 cup wheat flour
  • 1/2 cup shredded cabbage
  • 2 tablespoon finely chopped spring onions
  • 1/2 teaspoon ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon powdered turmeric
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped coriander leaves
  • 1/4 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Red chilli powder
  • salt as required
  • 1/4 cup shredded carrot
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped capsicum ( green pepper)
  • 2 tablespoon finely chopped fenugreek leaves( methi)
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic
  • 1/4 cup boiled,mashed peas
  • 1 chopped green chilli
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon refined oil

    Instructions

    Step 1 :Prepare the stuffing

  • Heat 1 teaspoon oil in a non-stick pan. Add carrot, cabbage, capsicum, spring onion, fenugreek leaves along with crushed ginger and garlic. Sprinkle salt and turmeric powder over it and saute over medium flame for 3 to 4 minutes or until vegetables are tender. Add boiled and mashed green peas to the mixture. Mix well and turn off the flame. Let the mixture cool down.

    • Step 2: Prepare the dough

  • Take 1 cup wheat flour, 1 teaspoon oil and salt in a bowl. Add the vegetable mixture, chopped coriander leaves, chopped green chilli, red chilli powder, garam masala powder and coriander powder. Mix them properly. Knead until smooth by adding small amounts of water. Grease its surface with little oil, cover and let it rest for 10 minutes
    • .

    Step 3 :Roll out parathas

  • After 10 minutes, divide the dough into 8 equal parts and make small balls. Take 1/2 cup of dry wheat flour in a plate for dusting. Take one dough ball, flatten it like a patty and dust-dry wheat flour over it. Roll out the paratha.

    • Step 4: Cook the paratha

  • Place it on a hot tava/griddle and cook over medium flame. When tiny bubbles start to rise on the surface, flip it and reduce the flame to low. Spread 1 teaspoon oil around the edges and spread over the paratha. Flip it again and spread 1/4 teaspoon oil around over it.

    • Step 5: Make more such parathas

  • Press it with a spatula and let it cook over medium flame. Cook until golden brown on both sides. Follow the same process for the remaining dough balls and you have a delicious and nutritious meal ready.

    • Step 6: Ready to be served

    Serve hot with a side dish of your choice like butter, curd, chutney or curry.

6- PANCAKES RECIPE :  

Prep Time Cook Time Total Time
30 mins 30 mins 60 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar, optional
  • 2 eggs
  • 1½ to 2 cups milk
  • 2 tablespoons melted and cooled butter (optional), plus unmelted butter for cooking, or use neutral oil

    Instructions

    Step 1 :

  • Heat a griddle or large skillet over medium-low heat. In a bowl, mix together dry ingredients. Beat eggs into 1½ cups milk, then stir in 2 tablespoons melted cooled butter, if using it. Gently stir this mixture into dry ingredients, mixing only enough to moisten flour; don't worry about a few lumps. If batter seems thick, add a little more milk.

    • Step 2:

  • Place a teaspoon or 2 of butter or oil on griddle or skillet. When butter foam subsides or oil shimmers, ladle batter onto griddle or skillet, making pancakes of any size you like. Adjust heat as necessary; usually, first batch will require higher heat than subsequent batches. Flip pancakes after bubbles rise to surface and bottoms brown, after 2 to 4 minutes.

    • Step 3 :

  • Cook until second side is lightly browned. Serve, or hold on an ovenproof plate in a 200-degree oven for up to 15 minutes.
